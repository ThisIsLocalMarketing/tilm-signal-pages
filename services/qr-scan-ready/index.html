Built for everyday customer moments

Make the next step easy to see and use.

The display should help a customer understand why to scan and what they can do next — without making them search through a website or guess where the QR leads.

One clear reason to scan
  • Lead with the useful action: view an offer, open a menu, book, request a quote, leave a review, or get directions.
  • Keep the prompt short enough to understand at a glance.
One useful destination
  • Connect the QR to the approved page or link that best supports the customer’s next step.
  • A Signal Page can be added when the existing website path is too scattered.
A format that fits the space
  • Counter displays, table pieces, window treatments, take-home cards, and other placements need different proportions and viewing distances.
  • The concept is shaped around where customers will actually encounter it.
